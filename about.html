About Us

We started our business in 2005 with trophies, plaques and engraving. We have since added direct to garment printing on apparel, post office box rentals, address stamps, embroidery, business cards, and laser engraving. We also offer screen printed vinyl for apparel and FedEx shipping services, with printing and faxing services. If you need it done, we can do it!

Our Team

John Thompson

Founder

I am a lifelong Franklin resident born and raised. As my kids were getting into activities around the Franklin community, I began coaching the various sports they were involved in.

It was in these roles where I discovered how generous and kind this community is to everyone who comes into contact with it. My youngest son had gotten into the sport of racing and in order to support him in this, we began making trophies for the racing series. What started in my living room as a part time venture, grew quickly.

In 2007 we purchased a local trophy shop, which began the now bricks and mortar portion of "A Trophy Business". I have a passion for serving the partnering relationships this business has made with the organizations across this diverse community.

Teri Bair

Co-Founder

I have lived in Franklin since 1983. I am an Army Brat. I have 3 children and 6 grandchildren. I worked at Alpine Electronics for 18 years, manufacturing, working on machines. Then on to Firefighter & EMT, and continued on to become a Bachelors Degree Registered Nurse since 2004.

We started our business in our garage. We were a racing family and needed extra income to supplement racing go karts parts and fees. We opened our business in 2005 with trophies and plaques.

We have since added several different services to our business, and moved into our own brick and mortar building. We are happy to continue our business in Franklin, and throughout the state of Indiana.
